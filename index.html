About Admission Office

Th admission Office evaluates the credentials of all applicants for admission to determine their qualifications to take the school entrance examinations. It administers Headwaters college Admission Test (HWCAT) on all applicants for enrollment to the different academic programs and submits the list of qualified applicants to the Office of the President for approval. The Admissions Office contantly coordinates with the different academic units on all matters pertaining to the student admission. It likewise conducts information campaigns to informthe public of the curricular programs and offering of the School.

Policy Guidelines

Freshmen applicants to the Headwaters College shall be admitted on the following basis:

* Performances in the HWCAT administered by the office of Admissions of the Headwaters College.

* Average of grades obtained in high school (1st and 2nd grading period of 4th year)

* Quotas and other requisites needed by the specific colleges set up by the respective Dean's Office.

* Qualifiers who intended to purse admission to the School are required to pay a nonrefundable reservation fee of Php 30,000.
